Welcome to the hobby page. All my hobbies related to the IT-Hemisphere will be described and presented here.


PC-Building

PC-Building allows me to express creativity and innovative thinking by carefully selecting which component most optimally fits for a situation and allows me to use said creativity to optimize my creation.

BLENDER

Blender involves 3D-Modelling, animating and rendering.
Because I deeply appreciate both expanding my knowledge in the IT hemisphere and learning new skills in order to use them in other projects, I took a liking towards 3D-Modelling. Like coding, 3D-Modelling is another way to turn concepts into reality and to nicely compliments any project involving both 3D-Modelling and coding.
Throughout 3 years I have both animated and 3D-Modelled meshes and animations most notably for games based on Unity.


C#

Programming involves writing software solutions for any problem or project at hand and is another way of communicating directly with computers. C# is one branch of the C family programming language, which is extensively used both professionally and by hobbyists for their projects.
Due to C# being another form of creative expression, being mainly based on logic and the groundwork of every other program and its purpose, I have decided for it to be my main professional direction.$ A lot of concepts, whether they be games, automations, applications or projects, can be implemented with c# or any equivalent coding language, each with their own specialization. With it also being more localized to a computer, like many of my other hobbies, I concentrate better and achieve a better workflow, due to it being my preferred way of working.
Throughout 2 years I have started a Unity course to lean the basics of both Unity and C# and worked on many projects within my current school.
